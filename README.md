# Adidas US Interactive Sales Dashboard 📊

An expert-level interactive sales dashboard built using **Excel/Power BI** to analyze Adidas' sales performance across the United States. This project transforms raw retail data into actionable business insights through advanced data visualization and analytical modeling.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview
This dashboard provides a 360-degree view of sales operations, helping stakeholders track profitability, unit volume, and regional performance. The analysis covers multiple dimensions including time (quarters), product categories, sales methods, and retailer performance.

### Key Metrics (KPIs):
* **Total Units Sold:** 2,478,861 Units
* **Total Operating Profit:** $332,134,761.45
* **Active Retailers:** 6 Major Distributors

---

## 🛠️ Features
The dashboard is designed with interactivity at its core:
* **Dynamic Filtering:** Integrated Slicers for **City** and **State** that update all visualizations in real-time.
* **Geographical Analysis:** A filled map visualizing operating profit by region.
* **Trend Tracking:** A multi-year line chart showing sales growth across 2020 and 2021.
* **Categorical Breakdown:** Analysis of profit per product (Apparel vs. Footwear) and Sales Method (Online, In-store, Outlet).
* **Retailer Benchmarking:** Performance comparison between top retailers like West Gear, Walmart, Amazon, and Foot Locker.

---

## 📂 Data Structure
The analysis is based on a comprehensive dataset (`project data.xlsx`) containing the following attributes:
* **Temporal:** Invoice Date (Year, Quarter, Month).
* **Geographic:** Region, State, City.
* **Product:** Product Name, Category (Men's/Women's Apparel & Footwear).
* **Financial:** Price per Unit, Units Sold, Total Sales, Operating Profit, Operating Margin.
* **Channel:** Sales Method (Online, Outlet, In-store).

---

## 📊 Visualizations Included
1.  **Profit Per Product:** Column chart comparing profitability across 6 product categories.
2.  **Sales Trend By Quarters:** Line chart highlighting seasonal peaks and growth trajectories.
3.  **Sales Method Distribution:** Doughnut chart showing the share of different sales channels.
4.  **Retailer Sales Leaderboard:** Bar chart ranking the top 6 retailers by total revenue.
5.  **Regional Profit Map:** Heat map identifying high-performing US states.

---

## ⚙️ Technical Implementation
* **Data Cleaning:** Handled missing values and standardized date formats.
* **Pivot Tables:** Used for complex data aggregation and multi-dimensional analysis.
* **DAX/Formulas:** Implemented custom calculations for Profit Margins and Distinct Counts.
* **UI/UX Design:** Dark-themed professional layout with rounded-corner containers for better readability.

---

## 📈 Insights Preview
* **Peak Growth:** Significant upward trend observed moving into Q3 and Q4 of 2021.
* **Top Channel:** In-store sales remain a dominant force, while online channels show consistent growth.
* **Product Leader:** Men's Street Footwear and Women's Apparel are top contributors to the total operating profit.
